## Infrastructure

### Production-ready UI

A flexible, customizable UI that adapts to your workflows and enables team collaboration, through OpenBB Apps.

- UI layer you control
- Adapt the UI to your team's specific workflows
- Build full applications from data visualization to AI workflows

### Connect all your data

Integrate proprietary, licensed, and public data, both structured and unstructured, in one secure, scalable AI-ready platform.

- All your data in one place
- Integrate proprietary, licensed, and public data
- Avoid vendor lock-in and legacy platforms

### Integrate your AI model

Integrate your own AI agents into a safe environment designed for experimentation and deployment.

- Integrate any AI model
- Connect the AI agent that supports your workflows best  
- Deploy AI in a secure environment with access to your data

### Run on-premises

Deploy OpenBB on-prem or in your private cloud for full control, security, and privacy over your data, AI models and prompts.

- Deploy securely on-premises or in your private cloud
- Run AI models locally to keep data within your infrastructure  
- Role-based access control
